About Café Galleria

Café Galleria is located in San Francisco, California and was founded with an idea to create a space that would not only serve great coffee but be an environment where friends and famiy can enjoy a third place as their home. With delicious coffee, great company, and food, what else could be even better? We'd love for you to come join us and unwind.

Our Coffee

Our espresso beans are sourced from Vietnam which is typically known for growing arabica coffee. We partnered with a family farm who also have passion for coffee. With our visions combined, we created Café Galleria to share this fresh and bright espresso beans to thousans of visitors like you.

Our Employees

We hand pick our employees, choosing those who have an excitement and love for coffee and people. With training and coffee knowledge, our baristas put a lot of care into your handcrafted beverage. Have a sip and enjoy!
